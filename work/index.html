About me

I'm Rens (Wouter) van der Heijden. Currently, I work at the Expleo Group. Before that, I was a Master student (Kerckhoffs track) at the University of Twente, Netherlands). I graduated cum laude (24 August, 2012) and won the ENIAC prize (for the year 2012, on November 23rd) with my thesis. It was later accepted at WiSec 2013 (see below).
